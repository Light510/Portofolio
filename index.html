About Me

I am an 11th-grade student at SMA Negeri 2 Cilacap. I have quite a lot of friends.


My hobbies include tinkering with my laptop and playing the guitar. Listening to music is part of my daily routine—there’s not a day without music for me.


My favorite band is Reality Club because their songs are amazing. I also enjoy music from many other bands and singers, such as HIVI!, Dewa 19, Sheila on 7, Bruno Mars, Juicy Luicy, and many more.

I love eating fried rice—it’s my favorite food. Besides that, I also enjoy eating ayam geprek, although I always order the non-spicy version.


I have a love for speed. I often ride fast on the road. Even though I stay within the speed limit, I consider myself quite fast. I’m also a fan of Formula 1 and MotoGP. To me, F1 and MotoGP racers are like superhumans who fear nothing.


That’s all about me.
